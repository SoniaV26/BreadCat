Summary

Have you ever wanted to eat out with friends, but didn’t know where to go? Do you constantly have to wonder if what you’re eating is good for your diet? We have solved this problem for you! Eating healthy with a dietary restriction is hard. Eating out with friends or family is difficult especially when one or more of you have a dietary restriction. It can feel awkward and frustrating trying to plan a meal with the people you care about and it’s even worse when you don’t know where to take them. We at BreadCat are trying to make healthy eating more accessible to people living their lives on the go! This app helps you filter for not just one dietary restriction, but multiple! Restaurants in the Tempe area are uploaded to our website to help you navigate your options easily. We have options to eliminate restaurants if they don’t meet your diet. Easily find out about all the different options available to you and what other users thought of their experience! You can follow your friends on BreadCat to see where they are eating and what they thought. You can also comment and rate the restaurants you eat at to let people know what you thought. There will be different dietary restrictions. You will be able to see how accommodating these restaurants are for these restrictions. In addition to this, you can also filter by restaurant style. You can also filter by rating. So many options! The world is your BreadCat!
